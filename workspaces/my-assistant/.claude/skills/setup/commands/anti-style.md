# /setup:anti-style

Populates `context/anti-ai-writing-style.md` through conversation.

## Explain first

"This file teaches me what NOT to write. It's the most effective way to stop AI-sounding responses. I'll show you common patterns — tell me which ones annoy you most, and add any of your own."

## Show the common list and ask them to react

Present these in groups and ask "Which of these bother you? (All? Some? None? Add your own)"

**Opening phrases:**
- "Great question!"
- "Certainly!"
- "Absolutely!"
- "Of course!"
- "I'd be happy to help with that."
- "I hope this finds you well."
- "Just wanted to reach out."

**Filler words and hedging:**
- "It's worth noting that…"
- "It's important to note…"
- "It goes without saying…"
- "Needless to say…"
- Hedging stacks: "perhaps it might possibly be worth maybe considering…"
- Three-adjective stacks: "a thoughtful, considered, deliberate approach"

**AI tells (words that signal AI writing):**
- "delve"
- "tapestry"
- "navigate the landscape"
- "in today's fast-paced world"
- "leverage"
- "robust"
- "streamline"
- "synergy"
- "paradigm"
- "holistic"
- "cutting-edge"

**Corporate-speak:**
- "circle back"
- "double-click on that"
- "take this offline"
- "move the needle"
- "at the end of the day"
- "going forward"

## Ask for additions

"Anything specific to your industry, background, or pet hates that I should add?"

## Collect a 'good vs bad' example

"Can you give me one example of writing that sounds like you, and one that sounds wrong? Even a sentence or two each."

## Write the file

```markdown
# Anti-AI writing style

Writing patterns I must never use.

## Banned opening phrases

[list from conversation]

## Filler and hedging patterns

[list]

## AI tells — words to avoid

[list]

## Corporate-speak

[list]

## [User's additions]

[list]

## Good vs bad

**Good (sounds like me):**
> [user's example]

**Bad (sounds like AI):**
> [user's example]

---

If a piece of writing could have come from any AI assistant or any corporate inbox, it's wrong.
```

Confirm: "I've saved your anti-style guide. This will actively shape how I write."
