# What is my-assistant?

**my-assistant** is a personal AI assistant for [Claude Cowork](https://claude.com/product/cowork). It knows who you are, how you write, and what you want help with — so you can stop repeating yourself every session.

## Who is it for?

Anyone who wants help with personal life — family logistics, home admin, planning, drafting messages, and keeping track of tasks — without setting everything up from scratch.

my-assistant:

- Remembers people, places, and projects between sessions
- Writes in your voice, not generic AI-speak
- Drafts messages and plans — but never sends or books without your say-so
- Works from files on your computer that you can read and edit

No coding required.

## How it works

Three steps:

1. **Install** — paste a prompt into Claude Code. It sets up your workspace.
2. **Setup** — run `/setup` from Claude Code at the repo root. my-assistant asks questions and saves your answers in your workspace.
3. **Use** — ask for help. my-assistant reads your context and memory each session.

## What you can do

| Command | When to use it |
|---------|----------------|
| `/setup` | First time, or when you want to update who you are and how you write |
| `/productivity:start` | Initialise your task list and memory |
| `/productivity:update` | Review open tasks and catch anything you've missed |
